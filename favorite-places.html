Our Favorite Places

Here are some of our favorite places!

Haleakala in Maui, Hawaii

Haleakala is certainly one of the most gorgeous views from anywhere on Maui. The top of the volcano pops up above the clouds and it feels like you are in another world when at the summit. We highly recommend that you visit this amazing landmark when you go to Maui!!!

Redwood National Forest, CA

The Redwood National Forest in Northern California is one of the most spiritual places we have been to. You can feel the age an power iminating from the massive 1000+ year old trees. It is also incredibly peaceful and beautiful. For a place to reflect and release tension, in our opinion, would be a prime choice.

Columbia River Gorge, Oregon

The Columbia River Gorge is amazingly beautiful and you can explore this beauty through an abundance of easy to difficult trails. You can merely take a day hike or backpack in for a longer period of time. Along the way of most of the trails, there will be a waterfall(s). If you want waterfalls, this is the place to go!

Rio De Janeiro, Brazil

We have never travelled to Rio, but we have heard there are outstanding beaches, excellent food, and Carnival which is a huge party where the entire city is dancing in the streets! We plan on visiting Rio just as soon as we get this code mastered.
